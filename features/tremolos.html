Single-note (bowed) tremolos

Verovio supports bowed tremolos encoded with <btrem> elements. The @measperf on the <bTrem> specifies the number of slashes to be displayed. If this is not given, Verovio looks at the @stem.mod on the first <note> or <chord> child.
