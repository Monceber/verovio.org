Cross-staff notation (work in progress)

Verovio supports basic cross-staff notation with displacement encoding. This means that the cross-staff elements (i.e., notes) have to be encoded in the logical staff with an additional @staff attribute indicating the cross-staff position. Currently, Verovio does not support the @layer attribute and expects that the same layer number to be present on the cross-staff. Clefs changes in the cross-staff are taken into account.
